Unawatuna

Unawatuna is a tropical paradise with golden sands, clear waters, snorkeling spots, and lush jungle fringes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Relax on soft golden sands with calm seas perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

Hidden jungle beaches provide privacy, scenic surroundings, and excellent photography spots.

Visit the Japanese Peace Pagoda offering panoramic views over the ocean and town.

Snorkel in clear waters near coral reefs teeming with tropical marine life.

Explore vibrant reef formations and marine biodiversity along the coastline.

Things to Do

  • Relax on the main beach.
  • Discover hidden jungle beaches.
  • Visit the Japanese Peace Pagoda.
  • Snorkel near coral reefs.
  • Photography of reefs and coastal scenery.

Best Time to Visit

December to April is ideal for sunbathing, snorkeling, and calm waters along the southern coast.
