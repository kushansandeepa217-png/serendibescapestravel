Ella

A charming mountain town surrounded by tea plantations, waterfalls, and breathtaking scenic viewpoints.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Nine Arch Bridge

Iconic railway bridge with stunning mountain views.

Little Adam's Peak

Popular hike offering panoramic vistas of the tea plantations.

Rawana Falls

Picturesque waterfall surrounded by lush forest.

Scenic Train Ride

Ride through rolling hills and tea estates for iconic photography.

Ella Rock

Challenging hike with rewarding 360° views of the region.

Things To Do

  • Hiking to Little Adam's Peak & Ella Rock
  • Explore tea plantations and factories
  • Visit waterfalls and natural pools
  • Enjoy scenic train rides through hills

Best Time to Visit

January to April – pleasant weather with clear skies and cooler temperatures.
