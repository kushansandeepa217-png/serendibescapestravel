Dambulla

Home to the world-famous Dambulla Cave Temple, this cultural city is rich in Buddhist history and picturesque landscapes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Cave Temple

Ancient rock temple with Buddhist statues and murals.

Golden Temple

Iconic golden Buddha statue at the entrance of the temple complex.

Local Market

Experience fresh produce, spices, and traditional crafts.

Viewpoint

Panoramic views over Dambulla town and surrounding farmlands.

Hurulu Eco Park

Wildlife sanctuary for elephants and diverse species.

Things To Do

  • Visit Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Explore local markets and villages
  • Wildlife spotting at Hurulu Eco Park
  • Hiking and sightseeing around the rock formations
