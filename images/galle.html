Galle

A historic coastal city with colonial architecture, charming streets, and scenic sunsets over the Indian Ocean.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Galle Fort

UNESCO World Heritage site with cobblestone streets and colonial architecture.

Galle Lighthouse

Historic lighthouse overlooking the fort and ocean.

Fort Walls

Perfect for sunset walks and panoramic coastal views.

Galle Museum

Explore regional history and cultural exhibits.

Jungle Beach

Secluded beach ideal for swimming and relaxing away from crowds.

Things To Do

  • Walk the historic fort walls
  • Explore boutique shops and cafes
  • Relax at Jungle Beach and oceanfront cafes
  • Visit museums and art galleries
