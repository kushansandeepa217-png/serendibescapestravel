Anuradhapura

The sacred city of ancient Sri Lanka, Anuradhapura is home to centuries-old ruins, stupas, and sacred Bodhi trees. Explore the rich heritage and spiritual significance of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Sri Maha Bodhi

One of the oldest living trees in the world, a sacred site for Buddhists.

Ruwanwelisaya

Massive stupa built by King Dutugemunu, known for its grandeur and historical importance.

Jetavanaramaya

One of the tallest stupas in the world, a remarkable feat of ancient engineering.

Kuttam Pokuna

Ancient twin bathing ponds showcasing the ingenuity of hydraulic engineering.

Isurumuniya

Famous for its rock carvings and beautiful architecture reflecting ancient artistry.

Things To Do

  • Visit ancient stupas and temples
  • Cycle around the sacred city
  • Explore archaeological ruins
  • Witness sunrise at Mihintale
