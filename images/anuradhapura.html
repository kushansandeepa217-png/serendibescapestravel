Anuradhapura

The sacred ancient capital of Sri Lanka, home to centuries-old stupas, monasteries, and the revered Sri Maha Bodhi.

About Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura, one of Sri Lanka’s most historically significant cities, is renowned for its ancient temples, massive stupas, and sacred Buddhist relics dating back over two millennia. This UNESCO World Heritage Site offers a peaceful spiritual atmosphere surrounded by picturesque reservoirs and archaeological wonders.

Key Attractions

Sri Maha Bodhi

Ruwanweliseya Stupa

Jetavanaramaya

Kuttam Pokuna

Isurumuniya Temple

Things to Do

  • Explore the ancient monastic ruins.
  • Watch sunset at the lakeside tanks.
  • Cycle across the heritage sites.
  • Visit revered temples and stupas.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Anuradhapura is **May to September**, during the dry season with pleasant warm weather.
