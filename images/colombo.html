Colombo

The bustling capital of Sri Lanka, Colombo blends colonial charm with modern energy, offering markets, temples, and cosmopolitan attractions.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Galle Face Green

Seaside promenade ideal for evening walks and local snacks.

Gangaramaya Temple

Famous Buddhist temple showcasing culture, art, and history.

Pettah Market

Vibrant local market for shopping, spices, and souvenirs.

One Galle Face

Modern shopping and entertainment complex by the sea.

Independence Square

Historic landmark surrounded by gardens and cultural events.

Things To Do

  • Explore colonial architecture
  • Shop at Pettah and modern malls
  • Relax at Galle Face Green
  • Visit temples and museums
