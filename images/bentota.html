Bentota

Luxury beach escapes and thrilling water sports await you in Bentota, a coastal town offering sun-soaked shores, river adventures, and vibrant culture.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Bentota Beach

Wide sandy beach ideal for sunbathing, swimming, and long walks.

Water Sports

Jet skiing, windsurfing, and other fun aquatic activities.

Bentara River

Enjoy river safaris to spot birds, mangroves, and serene landscapes.

Brief Garden

Beautifully landscaped estate showcasing art and tropical flora.

Turtle Hatchery

Visit sea turtles conservation projects and learn about their protection.

Things To Do

  • Relax on pristine beaches
  • Water sports and river safaris
  • Visit Brief Garden
  • Explore turtle hatcheries
