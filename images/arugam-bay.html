Arugam Bay

A paradise for surfers and nature lovers, Arugam Bay offers golden beaches, calm lagoons, and vibrant local culture along Sri Lanka’s east coast.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Main Beach

Popular for surfing, sunbathing, and vibrant beachside cafes.

Surf Point

Catch waves at one of Sri Lanka's premier surf spots.

Lagoon

Peaceful waters perfect for kayaking and birdwatching.

Elephant Sanctuary

Observe elephants in a natural, ethical sanctuary setting.

Whiskey Point

Famous scenic spot with dramatic coastal views.

Things To Do

  • Surfing and water sports
  • Relax at quiet beaches and lagoons
  • Explore local fishing villages
  • Wildlife spotting and photography

Best Time to Visit

April to October – ideal surf conditions and dry weather for beach activities.
