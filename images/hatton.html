Hatton

The heart of Sri Lanka’s tea country, surrounded by misty mountains, sprawling estates, and serene landscapes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Tea Estates

Endless rolling hills of tea gardens with breathtaking views.

Tea Factory Tour

Learn the art of tea production and taste premium Ceylon tea.

Adam’s Peak

A sacred mountain climb offering spectacular sunrise views.

Castlereagh Lake

Tranquil lake ideal for canoeing and peaceful walks.

Mountain Views

Panoramic vistas of lush valleys and waterfall-filled hills.

Things To Do

  • Tea estate tours and factory visits
  • Sunrise trek to Adam’s Peak
  • Boat rides on Castlereagh Lake
  • Mountain hikes and photography

Best Time to Visit

January to April – clear skies and cool weather in the hill country.
