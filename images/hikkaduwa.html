Hikkaduwa

A vibrant beach town known for coral reefs, snorkeling, nightlife, and golden beaches.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Coral Reef

Famous shallow reef ideal for snorkeling and marine life spotting.

Hikkaduwa Beach

Wide sandy beach perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

Tsunami Memorial

A moving tribute to victims of the 2004 tsunami.

Turtle Hatchery

Learn about turtle conservation and see rescued turtles.

Seafood Market

Fresh seafood and lively coastal atmosphere.

Things To Do

  • Snorkeling and diving at coral reef
  • Beach sports and surfing
  • Visit turtle conservation centers
  • Try fresh seafood at beachside restaurants
