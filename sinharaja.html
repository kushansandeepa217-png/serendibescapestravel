Sinharaja

Sinharaja Rainforest is a UNESCO World Heritage site with dense tropical jungle, endemic flora, wildlife, and pristine trails.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Walk along lush rainforest trails filled with vibrant trees, endemic plants, and dense canopy.

Discover hidden waterfalls for a refreshing dip amid serene jungle surroundings.

Birdwatching paradise with exotic and endemic bird species found only in Sri Lanka.

Hike various forest trails with expert guides to learn about the rainforest ecosystem.

Enjoy serene forest streams and natural pools for relaxation and photography.

Things to Do

  • Hike rainforest trails with guided tours.
  • Spot endemic bird and wildlife species.
  • Relax by forest streams and natural pools.
  • Explore hidden waterfalls and jungle paths.
  • Photography of tropical flora and fauna.

Best Time to Visit

January to April ensures drier conditions for trekking, wildlife spotting, and forest exploration.
