Love Songs of Ceylon

A 10-day honeymoon tour across cultural and coastal Sri Lanka — romance in every step.

Back to Tours

Overview

From beaches to the Cultural Triangle to mountain train rides — this honeymoon journey is crafted for romance, comfort and memorable moments.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo (25 minutes)
  • Negombo → Sigiriya (4.5 hours)
  • Sigiriya → Polonnaruwa → Sigiriya (3.5 hours)
  • Sigiriya → Kandy (3 hours)
  • Kandy — city tour (half day)
  • Kandy → Hatton (train journey)
  • Hatton — leisure
  • Hatton → Bentota (4.5 hours)
  • Bentota → Galle → Bentota (1.5 hours)
  • Bentota — beach & relaxation
  • Bentota → Airport (1.5 hours)
