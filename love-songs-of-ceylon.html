Love Songs of Ceylon

A romantic 10-day honeymoon tour through Sri Lanka’s cultural wonders and idyllic landscapes.

Why You’ll Love This Tour

  • Experience enchanting cultural sites and vibrant local life.
  • Indulge in culinary delights with curated dining experiences.
  • Stay in handpicked accommodations blending modern comfort and cultural charm.
  • Enjoy a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and romance.

Inclusions

  • Meals: Tailored to Your Preference
  • Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing
  • Accommodation: Hotel (10 nights)
  • Included Activities: Negombo City Tour, Ayurveda Therapy, Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Polonnaruwa, Elephant Safari, Temple of the Tooth, Cultural Dance, Royal Botanical Gardens, Udawatte Kele Trek, Train Ride, Galle Fort, Geoffrey Bawa’s Lunuganga

Itinerary

Arrival and transfer (25 minutes).

Kandy

Half-day city tour in Kandy.

Hatton

Day at leisure or optional exploration.

Hatton – Bentota

Drive 4.5 hours to Bentota.

Bentota – Galle – Bentota

Day trip to Galle (1.5 hours each way).

Bentota

Relax and enjoy beach or spa activities.

Bentota – Airport
