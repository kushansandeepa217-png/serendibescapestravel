Love Songs of Ceylon Tour Overview

This romantic journey is designed for couples and those seeking scenic beauty, cultural treasures, and serene beaches.

  • Experience Sri Lanka’s cultural highlights, including temples, tea plantations, and colonial towns.
  • Enjoy scenic train rides from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya and Nuwara Eliya to Ella.
  • Relax on pristine beaches with sunset views and optional whale watching.
  • Stay in premium romantic accommodations with curated meals and private transport.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Colombo – Romantic city dinner.
  • Colombo → Kandy – Temple & lake walk.
  • Kandy → Nuwara Eliya – Tea gardens & waterfalls.
  • Nuwara Eliya – Gregory Lake & Horton Plains.
  • Nuwara Eliya → Ella – Scenic train ride through hills.
  • Ella → Mirissa – Sunset beach stay.
  • Mirissa – Optional whale watching.
  • Mirissa → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Accommodation: 7 nights romantic hotels

Transport: Private A/C vehicle

Meals: Tailored to your preference

Activities: Scenic train rides, temple visits, beach relaxation, tea plantations, cultural tours, and romantic experiences.
