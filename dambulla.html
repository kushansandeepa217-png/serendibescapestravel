Dambulla

Home to Sri Lanka’s most iconic cave temple complex and stunning cultural views.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

The Dambulla Cave Temple complex showcases extraordinary Buddha statues, murals, and ancient craftsmanship.

The Golden Temple shines brightly with its massive golden Buddha statue and peaceful monastery grounds.

Dambulla’s hilltop viewpoints offer breathtaking panoramic scenes of the cultural triangle and forests.

The Dambulla Fruit Market is lively and full of fresh produce, colors, and authentic local culture.

Things to Do

  • Climb to the cave temples.
  • Discover ancient paintings.
  • Visit local temples and markets.
  • Explore nearby Sigiriya and Pidurangala.
  • Enjoy nature and hiking trails.

Best Time to Visit

December to April is the best period, with cool temperatures perfect for climbing and sightseeing.
