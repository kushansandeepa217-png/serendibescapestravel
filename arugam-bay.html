Arugam Bay

World-class surfing, golden shores, and a relaxed tropical atmosphere.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Arugam Bay Surf Point offers consistent waves, making it one of Asia‚Äôs top surfing destinations, attracting beginners and experts to its warm turquoise waters.

Arugam Bay Beach is a laid-back Sri Lankan surf haven known for its golden sands,warm waters and world-class waves.

Arugam Lagoon offers peaceful boat rides through mangroves, where visitors can spot crocodiles, birds, and stunning sunset reflections over calm waters.

An Arugam Bay elephant is often seen wandering near the lagoon or crossing coastal roads, adding a wild and iconic charm to the area's natural beauty.

Things to Do

  • Surf world-renowned waves.
  • Visit the historic Muhudu Maha Viharaya.
  • Safari at Kumana National Park.
  • Boat ride through mangrove lagoons.
  • Relax at beautiful beaches.

Best Time to Visit

The best season is May to October, offering ideal surfing conditions and sunny weather.
