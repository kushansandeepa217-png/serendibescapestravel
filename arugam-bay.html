Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay, a world-renowned surfing destination, offers pristine beaches, vibrant marine life, and a laid-back coastal vibe perfect for adventure and relaxation.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

The famous Arugam Bay Main Beach is ideal for surfing, sunbathing, and enjoying the stunning sunsets over the Indian Ocean, attracting visitors from around the globe.

Surf Point is the ultimate destination for surfers of all levels, offering consistent waves and a friendly local surf community in a scenic coastal setting.

The Arugam Bay Lagoon is a serene spot for kayaking, bird watching, and enjoying tranquil waterways lined with lush vegetation and abundant wildlife.

Nearby elephant sanctuaries allow visitors to observe these majestic animals in their natural habitat, offering an unforgettable wildlife experience close to the bay.

Whiskey Point is perfect for surfers seeking waves for beginners and intermediate levels, providing a relaxed atmosphere, local eateries, and beautiful coastal scenery.

Things to Do

  • Surfing at Main Beach and Whiskey Point.
  • Relax on pristine beaches and soak up the sun.
  • Kayak or paddle along Arugam Bay Lagoon.
  • Visit nearby elephant sanctuaries and wildlife reserves.
  • Enjoy local cuisine at beachfront cafes and restaurants.

Best Time to Visit

April to October is the prime season for surfing and enjoying calm beaches at Arugam Bay, with clear skies, warm water, and ideal wave conditions.
