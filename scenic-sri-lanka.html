Scenic Sri Lanka Tour

A snapshot of Sri Lanka’s landscapes, wildlife, and culture in 7 exciting nights.

Why You’ll Love This Trip

  • Explore diverse landscapes: cultural triangle, tea hills, Yala National Park, and coastal Galle.
  • Authentic Sri Lankan cuisine and cultural experiences.
  • Handpicked accommodations ensuring comfort and style.
  • Adventure, wildlife, and history combined for a complete journey.

Inclusions

  • Meals: Tailored to Your Preference
  • Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing
  • Accommodation: Hotel (7 nights)
  • Included Activities: Colombo City Tour, Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Polonnaruwa, Yala Safari, Cultural Dance, Nuwara Eliya, Galle Fort

Itinerary

Colombo City

Arrival and overnight stay.

Colombo – Sigiriya – Dambulla

Drive: 4.5 hours; sightseeing in Cultural Triangle.

Dambulla – Polonnaruwa – Dambulla

Drive: 3 hours; cultural tour in Kandy.

Nuwara Eliya – Yala

Drive: 4.5 hours; wildlife safari.

Yala – Galle

Drive: 4 hours; explore Galle Fort.

Galle – Airport

Drive: 2.5 hours; departure.
