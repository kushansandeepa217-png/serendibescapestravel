Scenic Sri Lanka Tour Overview

Explore the most breathtaking landscapes of Sri Lanka in a short but immersive tour, from rolling tea hills to wildlife safaris and cultural treasures.

  • Experience panoramic views of mountains, waterfalls, and lush tea plantations.
  • Visit ancient cities and cultural landmarks like Sigiriya and Polonnaruwa.
  • Wildlife safari in Yala National Park for leopard spotting.
  • Relax on southern beaches and explore Galle Fort.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Kandy – Arrival and city tour.
  • Kandy → Nuwara Eliya – Tea plantation visit.
  • Nuwara Eliya – Horton Plains excursion.
  • Nuwara Eliya → Ella – Scenic train ride.
  • Ella – Hike Little Adam’s Peak.
  • Ella → Yala – Safari adventure.
  • Yala → Galle – Explore Galle Fort.
  • Galle → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Accommodation: 7 nights comfortable hotels

Transport: Private air-conditioned vehicle

Meals: Tailored to your preference

Activities: City tours, safaris, scenic walks, cultural sites
