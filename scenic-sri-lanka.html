Scenic Sri Lanka

Enjoy a snapshot experience of Sri Lanka — temples, tea hills and wildlife in seven nights.

Back to Tours

Overview

One night in Colombo, two nights in the Cultural Triangle, then Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, Yala and Galle — a classic sampler of Sri Lanka.

Itinerary

  • Colombo City (1 hour)
  • Colombo → Sigiriya → Dambulla (4.5 hours)
  • Dambulla → Polonnaruwa → Dambulla (3 hours)
  • Dambulla → Kandy (3 hours)
  • Kandy → Nuwara Eliya (3 hours)
  • Nuwara Eliya → Yala (4.5 hours)
  • Yala → Galle (4 hours)
  • Galle → Airport (2.5 hours)
