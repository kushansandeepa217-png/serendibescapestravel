Sigiriya

The legendary Lion Rock Fortress — a UNESCO masterpiece of ancient engineering and art.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

A 200m ancient rock citadel with palace ruins.

Lion’s Paw

Massive stone lion paws marking the entrance to the summit climb.

Water Gardens

Advanced hydraulic gardens from the 5th century.

Mirror Wall

Ancient graffiti and polished stone pathways.

Sunset Views

Iconic landscapes featuring Pidurangala and the forest plains.

Things To Do

  • Climb the Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Explore the water gardens
  • Visit the frescoes and Mirror Wall
  • Climb Pidurangala for sunrise

Best Time to Visit

January to April — best weather for climbing and photography.
