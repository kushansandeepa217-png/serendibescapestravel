Sigiriya

Sigiriya, the Lion Rock, is an ancient fortress with rock paintings, water gardens, and panoramic views atop a massive rock plateau.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock for breathtaking views and ancient frescoes along the way.

The Lion's Paw is a massive stone gateway leading to the summit, symbolizing the fortressâ€™ grandeur.

Explore the beautifully restored water gardens at the base of the rock, showcasing ancient landscaping skills.

Admire the Mirror Wall with its ancient graffiti and preserved paintings of royal figures and courtiers.

End the day with a stunning sunset view from the rock summit, overlooking forests and villages.

Things to Do

  • Climb Sigiriya Rock and see frescoes.
  • Explore Lion's Paw and the summit.
  • Walk through water gardens and terraces.
  • Admire ancient Mirror Wall paintings.
  • Enjoy panoramic sunset views.

Best Time to Visit

December to April offers dry, sunny weather perfect for climbing, photography, and exploring gardens.
