Key Attractions

Gal Viharaya features majestic rock-cut Buddha statues carved into granite, a highlight of Polonnaruwa.

Parakrama Samudraya is a vast ancient reservoir surrounded by greenery and historical ruins.

Explore the remains of palaces, temples, and royal structures spread across the historic cityscape.

Marvel at intricate stone statues representing deities, kings, and mythological figures from the medieval period.

Visit the Polonnaruwa museum to view artifacts, inscriptions, and models of ancient architecture.

Things to Do

  • Visit Gal Viharaya rock-cut statues.
  • Walk around ancient city ruins.
  • Explore Parakrama Samudraya reservoir area.
  • Visit the museum for historical insights.
  • Photography of ancient structures and landscapes.

Best Time to Visit

December to April is ideal for dry weather, clear skies, and comfortable exploration of ancient sites.
