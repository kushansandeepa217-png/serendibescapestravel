Polonnaruwa

The ancient capital filled with UNESCO-listed ruins, stone carvings, and sacred temples.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Gal Viharaya

Iconic stone-carved Buddha statues.

Parakrama Samudraya

A massive ancient reservoir built by King Parakramabahu.

Ancient City Ruins

Historic palaces, temples, and stone structures.

Royal Statues

Carvings and statues celebrating ancient rulers.

Polonnaruwa Museum

Artifacts explaining the kingdom’s royal history.

Things To Do

  • Explore ancient ruins by bicycle
  • Visit archaeological sites
  • Discover stone carvings and temples
  • Relax near the Parakrama Samudraya reservoir

Best Time to Visit

July to September — warm, dry weather ideal for exploring.
