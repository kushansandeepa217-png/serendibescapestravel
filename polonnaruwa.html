Polonnaruwa

A UNESCO World Heritage site filled with ancient ruins, royal palaces, stupas, and statues.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Gal Vihara — iconic rock-carved Buddha statues.

Ancient royal palace and archaeological ruins.

Parakrama Samudraya is a massive ancient reservoir in Polonnaruwa, built by King Parakramabahu to harness water for agriculture and sustain the kingdom.

Things to Do

  • Explore ancient ruins and temples.
  • Cycle around the archaeological park.
  • Visit lakes and wildlife zones.
  • Photography and cultural tours.

Best Time to Visit

July to September — cooler mornings and less rain.
