Mirissa

A tropical gem famous for whale watching, golden beaches, and picture-perfect sunsets.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Mirissa Beach

Soft sands and turquoise waters perfect for relaxation.

Parrot Rock

A popular viewpoint at the edge of the ocean.

Whale Watching

One of the world’s best locations to spot blue whales.

Coconut Tree Hill

Instagram-famous cliff with sweeping ocean views.

Sunsets

Spectacular sunsets lighting up the coastline.

Things To Do

  • Whale watching tours
  • Surfing and snorkeling
  • Visit Coconut Tree Hill
  • Beach dining and nightlife

Best Time to Visit

November to April — ideal for whale watching and beach relaxation.
