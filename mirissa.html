Mirissa

Mirissa is a coastal paradise famous for whale watching, golden beaches, coconut hills, and relaxed beach vibes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Relax on golden sands, swim in clear waters, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of Mirissa Beach.

Climb Parrot Rock for panoramic views of Mirissa Bay and the surrounding coastline.

Go on a whale-watching tour to spot blue whales, dolphins, and other marine life in their natural habitat.

Explore Coconut Hill for a serene sunset, tropical views, and photography opportunities.

Witness breathtaking sunsets over the Indian Ocean, perfect for romantic evenings and peaceful moments.

Things to Do

  • Relax on Mirissa Beach and swim.
  • Climb Parrot Rock for panoramic views.
  • Go whale and dolphin watching tours.
  • Explore Coconut Hill for scenic sunset views.
  • Photography and leisure along the coastline.

Best Time to Visit

November to April provides calm seas, clear skies, and excellent conditions for beach activities and whale watching.
