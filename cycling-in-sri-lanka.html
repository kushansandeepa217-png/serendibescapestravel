Cycling in Sri Lanka

Discover Sri Lanka’s hidden treasures by bike on this exhilarating 6-day journey from Negombo to Kandy.

Why You’ll Love This Cycling Tour

  • Pedal through ancient ruins, bustling markets, and serene villages.
  • Experience breathtaking landscapes, from misty mountains to pristine coastlines.
  • Savor authentic Sri Lankan cuisine along the route.
  • Travel sustainably with eco-friendly cycling exploration.

Inclusions

  • Meals: Tailored to Your Preference
  • Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing
  • Accommodation: Hotel (5 nights)
  • Included Activities: Negombo City Tour, Dambulla Golden Cave Temple, Polonnaruwa, Elephant Safari, Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Temple of the Tooth, Peradeniya Botanical Gardens

Itinerary

Arrival in Negombo

Transfer from Airport to hotel. Light cycling around Negombo lagoon and welcome dinner.

Negombo – Kurunegala – Dambulla

Drive: 3 hours, Cycle: 5 hours. Explore rural villages and local markets along the way.

Dambulla – Polonnaruwa – Dambulla

Drive: 3 hours, Cycle: 3 hours. Visit UNESCO heritage sites and ancient ruins.

Dambulla – Sigiriya – Kandy

Drive: 3 hours, Cycle: 2 hours. Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress and ride through scenic landscapes.

Kandy

Cycle: 3 hours. Explore Temple of the Tooth and Kandy City at a leisurely pace.

Kandy – Airport

Drive: 4 hours. Depart from Colombo Airport after breakfast.
