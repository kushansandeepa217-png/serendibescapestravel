Cycling in Sri Lanka

How you journey is as important as what you see — cycle through Sri Lanka’s hidden treasures on this active adventure.

Back to Tours

Overview

Starting from Negombo, cycle through Dambulla and up to Kandy with two nights in each town — six days of cycling with support vehicle and restful stays.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo (25 minutes)
  • Negombo → Kurunegala → Dambulla (Drive + Cycle)
  • Dambulla → Polonnaruwa → Dambulla (Cycle + Drive)
  • Dambulla → Sigiriya → Kandy (Drive + Cycle)
  • Kandy — cycling around city
  • Kandy → Airport (Drive)
