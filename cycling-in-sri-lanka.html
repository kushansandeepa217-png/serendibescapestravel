Cycling in Sri Lanka Tour

How you journey in search of Sri Lanka’s hidden treasures is just as important as seeing them. Being adventurous at heart, cycling is an exhilarating way to experience Sri Lanka.

Starting from Negombo, set off to the cultural hub of Dambulla and then up the mountains to Kandy. Spend two nights at each town to rest, recover and explore before returning to Colombo – altogether six days of excitement.

This tour is best suited for those of moderate fitness, regardless of age.

Why You’ll Love this Cycling Tour Package

  • Pedal through ancient ruins, bustling markets and serene villages, encountering the heart of Sri Lanka up close.
  • Uncover breathtaking scenery as you cycle through diverse landscapes, from misty mountains to pristine coastlines.
  • Savor the vibrant flavors of Sri Lankan cuisine, sampling local delicacies during your journey.
  • Contribute to sustainable tourism by choosing an eco-friendly mode of exploration.

Is This Trip Right For You?

  • While suitable for active enthusiasts, the itinerary is designed with a moderate pace for varying fitness levels.
  • Ideal for those eager to delve into Sri Lanka’s diverse culture and authentic experiences.
  • Perfect for solo travelers, couples, or friends seeking a shared adventure.
  • If you relish the outdoors, this trip integrates nature and adventure seamlessly.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo – Arrival and leisure.
  • Negombo → Anuradhapura → Kandalama – Sacred city cycling.
  • Kandalama → Sigiriya → Polonnaruwa – Forest & ruins ride.
  • Dambulla → Matale → Kandy – Spice gardens & temples.
  • Kandy → Bandarawela → Ella – Tea estates & waterfalls.
  • Ella → Galle – Scenic coastal descent.
  • Galle → Colombo – Colonial heritage & beaches.
  • Colombo → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Meals: Tailored to Your Preference

Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing

Accommodation: 5 Nights in Premium Hotels

Included Activities: City Tour of Negombo, Dambulla Golden Cave Temple, Polonnaruwa, Elephant Safari, Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Temple of the Tooth Relic, Peradeniya Botanical Gardens
