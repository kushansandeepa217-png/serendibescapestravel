Geoffrey Bawa Architecture Tour

A curated journey through the masterpieces of Sri Lanka’s most iconic architect.

Back to Tours

Tour Overview

Explore the architectural genius of Geoffrey Bawa while enjoying the natural beauty and cultural richness of Sri Lanka. This tour covers Bawa’s iconic works along with serene landscapes, colonial architecture, and boutique hotel experiences.

  • Visit Lunuganga, Brief Garden, Kandalama Hotel, and Lighthouse Hotel.
  • Experience guided architectural walks and cultural tours.
  • Enjoy scenic drives through Sri Lanka’s diverse landscapes.
  • Stay in hotels inspired by Bawa’s architecture.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Colombo – Arrival & Bawa City Office.
  • Colombo → Bentota – Lunuganga Estate visit.
  • Bentota – Brief Garden & surrounding landscapes.
  • Bentota → Kandalama – Check-in Kandalama Hotel.
  • Kandalama → Galle – Lighthouse Hotel & Galle Fort.
  • Galle → Colombo – Explore Bawa residences.
  • Colombo – Guided architecture walk & cultural visits.
  • Colombo → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Architecture Highlights: Lunuganga, Brief Garden, Kandalama, Lighthouse Hotel

Transport: Private air-conditioned vehicle

Accommodation: 7 nights boutique or Bawa-inspired hotels

Meals: Tailored to your preference

Activities: Guided architectural tours, cultural visits, scenic drives, nature walks
