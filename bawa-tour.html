Courtyards & Verandahs — Geoffrey Bawa

Sri Lanka’s most renowned architect Geoffrey Bawa redefined and influenced South-East Asian architecture. This tour celebrates his creative genius.

Back to Tours

Overview

Begin your journey with two nights in the Cultural Triangle at one of Bawa’s iconic hotels, then travel south for a night up in the mountains of Ella before arriving on the coast of Galle at one of Bawa’s most celebrated creations.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo (25 minutes)
  • Negombo → Anuradhapura → Kandalama (4.5 hours)
  • Kandalama → Sigiriya → Polonnaruwa → Kandalama (3.5 hours)
  • Dambulla → Matale → Kandy (3 hours)
  • Kandy → Bandarawela → Ella (4.5 hours)
  • Ella → Galle (5.5 hours)
  • Galle → Colombo (2.5 hours)
  • Colombo → Airport (45 minutes)

Included Activities

Negombo city tour, visit to fish market, Anuradhapura, Heritance Kandalama (Bawa-designed), Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Polonnaruwa, Matale Heritage Centre, Temple of the Tooth, Nine Arch Bridge, Rawana Falls, Galle Fort, Lunuganga and Colombo city tour.
