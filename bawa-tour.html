Courtyards & Verandahs – Geoffrey Bawa Tour

Experience Sri Lanka through the masterpieces of Geoffrey Bawa, the island’s most renowned architect.

Tour Overview

This tour is dedicated to Geoffrey Bawa’s creative genius. You will visit and stay at places he conceptualised or inspired: start with two nights in the cultural triangle, then travel to the mountains of Ella, and finish with a night on the coast of Galle at one of Bawa’s iconic creations.

Immerse yourself in the architecture, landscapes, and culture of Sri Lanka with this curated journey.

Why You’ll Love This Geoffrey Bawa Tour

  • Explore charming courtyards and verandahs that resonate with Sri Lanka’s rich history.
  • Indulge in breathtaking landscapes and serene retreats that redefine relaxation.
  • Experience the warmth of personalized hospitality at every stop.
  • Delight your taste buds with a gastronomic journey from traditional delicacies to gourmet experiences.
  • Perfect for photography enthusiasts and cultural explorers.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Airport – Negombo (25 minutes)
  2. Day 2: Negombo – Anuradhapura – Kandalama (4.5 hours)
  3. Day 3: Kandalama – Sigiriya – Polonnaruwa – Kandalama (3.5 hours)
  4. Day 4: Dambulla – Matale – Kandy (3 hours)
  5. Day 5: Kandy – Bandarawela – Ella (4.5 hours)
  6. Day 6: Ella – Galle (5.5 hours)
  7. Day 7: Galle – Colombo (2.5 hours)
  8. Day 8: Colombo – Airport (1 hour)

Included Activities

  • Negombo City Tour & Fish Market Visit
  • Sacred City of Anuradhapura
  • Tour of Bawa Designed Hotel Heritance Kandalama
  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress & Polonnaruwa Ancient City
  • Matale Heritage Centre & Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Bandarawela & Ella attractions including Nine Arch Bridge, Rawana Falls
  • Ruhuna University, Matara Post Office, Dutch Fort of Galle
  • Geoffrey Bawa’s Lunuganga Country House & Colombo City Tour
