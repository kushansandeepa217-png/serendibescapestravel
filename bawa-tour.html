Courtyards & Verandahs – Geoffrey Bawa Tour

Explore the masterpieces of Geoffrey Bawa across Sri Lanka's cultural, mountain, and coastal landscapes.

Tour Overview

This 8-day journey takes you through Sri Lanka’s most iconic locations influenced by Geoffrey Bawa, including cultural triangle hotels, mountain retreats in Ella, and coastal gems in Galle. Immerse yourself in architecture, landscapes, and culture like never before.

Why You’ll Love This Tour

Authentic cultural immersion through architecture and landscapes.
Breathtaking scenery from mountains to the coast.
Warm personalized hospitality at every stop.
Gastronomic experiences from traditional to gourmet.
Perfect for photography enthusiasts.

Itinerary

Airport – Negombo

Arrival and transfer to Negombo, 25 minutes.

Negombo – Anuradhapura – Kandalama

Travel 4.5 hours exploring cultural triangle and historic sites.

Kandalama – Sigiriya – Polonnaruwa – Kandalama

3.5 hours visiting ancient architecture and heritage sites.

Dambulla – Matale – Kandy

3 hours exploring temples and local culture.

Kandy – Bandarawela – Ella

4.5 hours with scenic train rides and mountain stops.

Ella – Galle

5.5 hours traveling to the coastal region.

Galle – Colombo

2.5 hours exploring Galle Fort and city highlights.

Colombo – Airport

1 hour transfer to airport for departure.
