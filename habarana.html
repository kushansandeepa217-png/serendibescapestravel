Habarana

The gateway to Sri Lanka’s wildlife safaris, lakes, and cultural wonders.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Minneriya National Park is home to elephants, deer, birds, and incredible safari landscapes.

Habarana Lake is peaceful and scenic, perfect for sunset walks and birdwatching.

Village tours showcase traditional Sri Lankan culture, food, and rural lifestyles.

The Elephant Gathering is one of the world’s largest annual wildlife spectacles.

Things to Do

  • Go on wildlife safaris.
  • Take a traditional village tour.
  • Visit Sigiriya and Dambulla nearby.
  • Walk around lakes and nature trails.
  • Spot elephants in the wild.

Best Time to Visit

June to September is best to witness the famous Elephant Gathering.
