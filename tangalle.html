Tangalle

Tangalle is a serene coastal town with secluded beaches, turquoise waters, and lush lagoons perfect for relaxation and water activities.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Wide sandy beaches with calm waters for swimming, sunbathing, and evening strolls.

Historic lighthouse offering panoramic views of the Indian Ocean and surrounding coastline.

Kalametiya Lagoon is rich in birdlife and mangroves, ideal for nature tours and photography.

Explore peaceful lagoons with small boat rides and scenic water views.

Experience breathtaking sunsets over the ocean, perfect for photography and relaxing evenings.

Things to Do

  • Relax on secluded beaches.
  • Visit Tangalle Lighthouse.
  • Birdwatch at Kalametiya Lagoon.
  • Take boat rides in lagoons.
  • Capture stunning sunsets.

Best Time to Visit

April to September is ideal for calm seas, sunny weather, and water activities along the southern coast.
