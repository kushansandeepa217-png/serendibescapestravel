Tangalle

A peaceful southern escape with palm-fringed beaches, lagoons, and natural beauty.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Uncrowded beaches perfect for relaxing.

Lagoon tours and kayaking.

The Tangalle Lighthouse stands on a rocky promontory overlooking the Indian Ocean, offering sweeping coastal views and a peaceful, windswept atmosphere.

Things to Do

  • Beach relaxation.
  • Kayaking and lagoon safaris.
  • Temple visits.
  • Whale watching (nearby Mirissa).

Best Time to Visit

December to April for perfect coastal weather.
