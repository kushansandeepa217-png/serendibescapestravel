Kitulgala

The adventure capital of Sri Lanka offering rafting, canyoning, waterfalls, and rainforest hikes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

White-water rafting on Kelani River.

Waterfalls perfect for canyoning and natural pools.

Rainforest hikes filled with wildlife and lush greenery.

Things to Do

  • Water rafting.
  • Canyoning and waterfall jumps.
  • Birdwatching.
  • Rainforest trekking.
  • Rock sliding.

Best Time to Visit

Best from December to April when river conditions are ideal for adventure sports.
