Trincomalee

Famed for its pristine beaches, ancient temples, and world-class diving sites.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Koneswaram Temple

A sacred Hindu temple perched on a dramatic cliff.

Nilaveli Beach

One of Sri Lanka’s most beautiful calm-water beaches.

Pigeon Island

Famous for coral reefs and snorkeling with reef sharks.

Fort Frederick

A colonial-era fort built by the Portuguese.

Natural Harbor

One of the world’s deepest natural harbors.

Things To Do

  • Snorkeling at Pigeon Island
  • Relaxing at Nilaveli Beach
  • Temple visits
  • Dolphin and whale watching

Best Time to Visit

April to September — calm seas and perfect beach weather.
