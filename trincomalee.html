Trincomalee

Pristine blue beaches, snorkeling paradise, temples, and historical wonders.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Nilaveli Beach — one of Sri Lanka’s best white-sand beaches.

Pigeon Island — corals, sharks, sea turtles.

Koneswaram Temple overlooking the ocean cliffs.

Things to Do

  • Snorkeling at Pigeon Island.
  • Beach relaxation.
  • Temple visits.
  • Whale watching (seasonal).

Best Time to Visit

May to September — East Coast peak season.
