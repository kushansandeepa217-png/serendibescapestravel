Trincomalee

Trincomalee, an east coast gem, features golden beaches, historic forts, whale watching, and pristine coral reefs.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Koneswaram Temple perched on Swami Rock with spiritual and cultural significance.

Nilaveli Beach offers pristine sands, calm waters, and snorkeling opportunities.

Pigeon Island National Park is a snorkeling and diving paradise with coral reefs and marine life.

Explore Fort Frederick, an ancient fort with panoramic views of the ocean and town.

Things to Do

  • Visit Koneswaram Temple.

Best Time to Visit

May to September ensures calm waters, ideal for snorkeling, diving, and beach activities on the east coast.
