Kandy

The cultural capital famed for the Temple of the Tooth, scenic lake, and heritage traditions.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic — a Buddhist treasure.

Kandy Lake — peaceful walks with scenic views.

Royal Botanical Gardens — home to orchids and exotic trees.

Things to Do

  • Temple rituals and cultural visits.
  • Walk around Kandy Lake.
  • Explore Peradeniya Gardens.
  • Watch traditional dance shows.
  • Visit tea factories nearby.

Best Time to Visit

December to April is the most pleasant season with mild weather.
