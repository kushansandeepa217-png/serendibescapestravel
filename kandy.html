Kandy

The cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to sacred temples, lush gardens, and breathtaking hill-country scenery.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Temple of the Tooth

The holiest Buddhist temple in Sri Lanka, housing the sacred relic of the Buddha.

Royal Botanical Gardens

Vast gardens filled with orchids, giant trees, and exotic flora.

Kandy Lake

A peaceful lake in the heart of the city, ideal for scenic walks.

Cultural Dance Show

Traditional Kandyan dance performances showcasing rich heritage.

Udawattakele Sanctuary

A peaceful forest reserve great for birdwatching and nature walks.

Things To Do

  • Visit the Temple of the Tooth
  • Enjoy botanical gardens and nature parks
  • Witness traditional cultural dances
  • Explore tea museums and heritage sites
