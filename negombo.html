Negombo

Negombo is a coastal city near Colombo with lively beaches, canals, historic churches, seafood markets, and rich Dutch heritage.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Negombo Beach is perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying lively beachside caf√©s and restaurants.

Negombo Lagoon offers tranquil boat rides, birdwatching, and scenic views of mangroves and fishing villages.

The Fish Market is bustling with daily catches, offering an authentic glimpse into local fishing culture.

Historic churches display Portuguese and Dutch architectural heritage, with serene interiors and colorful facades.

Take a walk along the Dutch Canal for picturesque views of waterways, boats, and traditional lifestyle along the banks.

Things to Do

  • Relax on Negombo Beach and enjoy the sun.
  • Boat rides and birdwatching at Negombo Lagoon.
  • Visit the bustling Fish Market for local seafood.
  • Explore historic churches and cultural sites.
  • Walk along the Dutch Canal for scenic views.

Best Time to Visit

December to April provides dry weather, calm seas, and comfortable temperatures for beach, lagoon, and sightseeing activities.
