Negombo

A lively coastal town with beaches, canals, seafood markets, and colonial landmarks.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Negombo Beach

Golden sands and vibrant beachfront lifestyle.

Negombo Lagoon

A tranquil lagoon ideal for boat rides and fishing.

Fish Market

One of Sri Lanka’s largest fresh seafood markets.

Colonial Churches

Historic Portuguese and Dutch churches.

Dutch Canal

A scenic waterway reflecting Negombo’s colonial past.

Things To Do

  • Boat rides on Negombo Lagoon
  • Explore fish markets and seafood restaurants
  • Visit colonial churches and canals
  • Relax at beachside resorts

Best Time to Visit

December to April — best beach weather and calm seas.
