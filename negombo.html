Negombo

A lively coastal town close to the airport with beaches, seafood, and colonial heritage.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Negombo Beach — popular for water sports and sunset views.

Negombo Lagoon — boat rides and mangrove tours.

Dutch Fort and colonial-era architecture.

Things to Do

  • Beach relaxation and watersports.
  • Lagoon boat safaris.
  • Fresh seafood dining.
  • Explore colonial sites.

Best Time to Visit

December to April — dry season with warm beach-friendly weather.
