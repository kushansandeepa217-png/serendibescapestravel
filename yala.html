Yala

Yala National Park, famous for leopards and elephants, offers thrilling safaris, diverse habitats, and pristine coastline.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Spot elusive leopards in their natural habitat on exciting safari drives.

Observe wild elephants roaming across the park's forests and open plains.

Take guided safari jeep tours through savannah, lagoons, and forests.

Birdwatching opportunities with endemic and migratory species throughout the park.

Explore pristine coastal areas within the park for scenic views and photography.

Things to Do

  • Safari drives to spot leopards and elephants.
  • Birdwatching in diverse habitats.
  • Photograph scenic coastlines.
  • Explore lagoons and forest trails.
  • Enjoy guided wildlife tours.

Best Time to Visit

February to July provides excellent safari conditions, wildlife sightings, and sunny weather.
