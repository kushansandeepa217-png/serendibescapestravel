Yala

Home to Sri Lanka’s highest density of leopards, stunning coastal landscapes, and diverse wildlife.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Leopard Sightings

Yala is world-famous for its high density of wild leopards.

Wild Elephants

Large herds roam freely across the park’s vast open plains.

Safari Drives

Exciting jeep safaris offering up-close animal encounters.

Birdlife

Over 200 species including eagles, owls, hornbills & migrants.

Yala Coastline

Rugged beaches with breathtaking ocean views.

Things To Do

  • Early morning safari adventure
  • Evening safari drive with photographers
  • Birdwatching & nature trails
  • Visit Yala coastline lookout points

Best Time to Visit

February to September — driest months with improved chances to spot leopards and other wildlife.
