Jaffna

A cultural treasure in northern Sri Lanka known for temples, islands, history, and authentic Tamil culture.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

The historic Jaffna Fort overlooking the serene lagoon.

Nallur Kandaswamy Temple — a major Hindu spiritual landmark.

Delft Island — wild ponies and unique coral landscapes.

Things to Do

  • Explore Jaffna Fort.
  • Temple visits and cultural walks.
  • Visit islands like Delft and Nainativu.
  • Enjoy signature Jaffna cuisine.

Best Time to Visit

Best from January to July — warm and dry weather perfect for sightseeing.
