Jaffna

A vibrant northern city rich in culture, history, spirituality, and unspoiled coastal beauty.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Jaffna Fort

Dutch-built coastal fort with historical significance.

Nallur Temple

The most sacred Hindu temple in the region.

Casuarina Beach

Peaceful and shallow waters ideal for swimming.

Delft Island

A remote island with wild horses and ancient ruins.

Keerimalai Springs

Sacred freshwater springs by the sea.

Things To Do

  • Visit Nallur Temple and Hindu religious sites
  • Explore Delft Island
  • Relax at Casuarina Beach
  • Discover Jaffna’s cuisine and markets

Best Time to Visit

January to August – dry weather perfect for sightseeing and beach visits.
