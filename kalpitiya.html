Kalpitiya

A coastal paradise famous for dolphin watching, kitesurfing, and pristine lagoons.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Dolphin Watching

One of the best dolphin-watching spots in Sri Lanka.

Kitesurfing

World-class kitesurfing conditions at Kalpitiya Lagoon.

Bar Reef

Sri Lanka’s largest coral reef ideal for snorkeling.

Kalpitiya Fort

Historic Dutch fort overlooking the lagoon.

Kalpitiya Lagoon

Serene waters ideal for boat rides and relaxation.

Things To Do

  • Dolphin and whale watching
  • Kitesurfing and water sports
  • Snorkeling at Bar Reef
  • Explore secluded coastal villages

Best Time to Visit

November to April – calm seas and peak kitesurfing season.
