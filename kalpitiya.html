Kalpitiya

Kalpitiya is a coastal paradise known for dolphins, kite surfing, pristine beaches, and mangrove lagoons in northwest Sri Lanka.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Take a boat tour to spot playful dolphins in the turquoise waters of Kalpitiya.

Kalpitiya is renowned worldwide for kite surfing with ideal winds and flat lagoons.

Bar Reef offers spectacular snorkeling and diving with vibrant corals and marine life.

Explore Kalpitiya Fort, a colonial-era fortification with historical significance and ocean views.

Discover peaceful lagoons surrounded by mangroves, perfect for kayaking and nature photography.

Things to Do

  • Dolphin and whale watching tours.
  • Kite surfing or water sports at the lagoons.
  • Snorkeling and diving at Bar Reef.
  • Visit Kalpitiya Fort and coastal landmarks.
  • Kayaking through the mangrove lagoons.

Best Time to Visit

May to September is perfect for dolphin spotting, kite surfing, and calm waters for lagoons and marine activities.
