Kalpitiya

A paradise for kite surfing, dolphin watching, lagoons, and untouched beaches.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

One of the best spots in Asia for dolphin watching.

World-class kitesurfing lagoons ideal for beginners and pros.

Uncrowded beaches offering peaceful sunset views.

Things to Do

  • Kitesurfing and windsurfing.
  • Dolphin and whale watching.
  • Boat rides in the lagoon.
  • Relax on secluded beaches.

Best Time to Visit

Main season for wind sports: May–October and December–February.
