Southern Riviera Tour

Luxury 10-night journey exploring the Southern and cultural heart of Sri Lanka.

Why You’ll Love This Trip

  • Golden beaches, lush tea plantations, wildlife safaris, and cultural heritage.
  • Gourmet local flavors and unique culinary experiences.
  • Handpicked boutique villas offering comfort and style.
  • Adventure, relaxation, and luxury seamlessly combined.

Inclusions

  • Meals: Tailored to Your Preference
  • Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing
  • Accommodation: Hotel (10 nights)
  • Included Activities: Negombo Fishing Village, Anuradhapura, Mihintale, Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Polonnaruwa, Tea Estates, Yala Safari, Galle Fort, Turtle Hatchery

Itinerary

Airport – Negombo

Arrival and check-in. Drive: 30 minutes.

Negombo – Anuradhapura

Drive: 4 hours; explore ancient city and temples.

Anuradhapura

Full day sightseeing of heritage sites.

Anuradhapura – Sigiriya

Drive: 1.5 hours; climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress.

Sigiriya – Hatton

Drive: 5 hours; travel to tea plantations in the mountains.

Hatton

Day at leisure; explore nearby attractions.

Hatton – Yala

Drive: 6.5 hours; afternoon safari in Yala National Park.

Yala

Safari and relaxation at the park.

Yala – Bentota

Drive: 4 hours; coastal exploration and leisure.

Bentota

Relax on beaches or optional activities.

Bentota – Airport

Drive: 2 hours; departure.
