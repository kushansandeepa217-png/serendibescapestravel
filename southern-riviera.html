Southern Riviera Tour Overview

Discover the golden beaches, lush jungles, and cultural heritage of southern Sri Lanka in this immersive tour.

  • Relax on pristine beaches from Hikkaduwa to Tangalle.
  • Experience wildlife safaris in Yala National Park.
  • Explore ancient cities and cultural sites like Galle Fort.
  • Enjoy luxurious beachfront accommodation and local seafood delights.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo – Arrival & evening relaxation.
  • Negombo → Anuradhapura – Ancient city tour.
  • Anuradhapura → Sigiriya – Explore Sigiriya Rock Fortress.
  • Sigiriya → Hatton – Tea plantations & scenic views.
  • Hatton – Horton Plains and nature walks.
  • Hatton → Yala – Safari adventure.
  • Yala – Wildlife & nature exploration.
  • Yala → Bentota – Beach leisure.
  • Bentota – Water sports & relaxation.
  • Bentota → Negombo → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Accommodation: 10 nights luxury or beachfront hotels

Transport: Private air-conditioned vehicle

Meals: Tailored to your preference

Activities: Safaris, cultural tours, beach relaxation, nature walks
