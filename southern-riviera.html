Southern Riviera

A comprehensive 10-night luxury experience across Sri Lanka’s south and historic heartlands.

Back to Tours

Overview

This 10-night itinerary covers Negombo, the Cultural Triangle, tea country, jungle safaris and the southwest coast — staying at boutique properties and enjoying local cuisine and culture.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo (30 minutes)
  • Negombo → Anuradhapura (4 hours)
  • Anuradhapura — exploration
  • Anuradhapura → Sigiriya (1.5 hours)
  • Sigiriya → Hatton (5 hours)
  • Hatton — tea country
  • Hatton → Yala (6.5 hours)
  • Yala — safari
  • Yala → Bentota (4 hours)
  • Bentota — beach & leisure
  • Bentota → Airport (2 hours)
