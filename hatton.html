Hatton

The heart of Sri Lanka’s tea country, surrounded by misty mountains and plantations.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Vast tea estates offer scenic walks, factory tours, and breathtaking views across misty valleys.

Adam’s Peak is a historic pilgrimage climb offering sunrises that light up the entire mountain range.

Hatton is surrounded by waterfalls such as Laxapana and Aberdeen, making it a nature lover’s paradise.

Castlereigh Reservoir offers peaceful lakeside views, scenic trails, and luxury boutique stays.

Things to Do

  • Explore tea plantations.
  • Climb Adam’s Peak.
  • Visit iconic waterfalls.
  • Stay at boutique colonial bungalows.
  • Enjoy photography and nature walks.

Best Time to Visit

January to May offers the clearest skies for hiking and mountain scenery.
