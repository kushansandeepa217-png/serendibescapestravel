Udawalawe

Udawalawe National Park is a wildlife haven featuring elephants, birds, and safari adventures in lush landscapes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Spot wild elephants roaming freely in their natural habitat during safari drives.

Experience thrilling jeep safaris with expert guides through the national park.

Udawalawe Reservoir attracts birds and wildlife, offering scenic views and photo opportunities.

Birdwatchers can enjoy spotting endemic and migratory species around the park.

Take in the lush landscapes, grassy plains, and tropical surroundings of the park.

Things to Do

  • Elephant spotting in natural habitats.
  • Jeep safari adventure.
  • Birdwatching around the reservoir.
  • Photography of wildlife and landscapes.
  • Relax and enjoy natural scenery.

Best Time to Visit

May to September is ideal for safaris, wildlife sightings, and dry weather exploration.
