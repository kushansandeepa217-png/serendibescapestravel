Experiential East

Immerse yourself in the vibrant hues of East Sri Lanka – history, nature, and culture all in one unforgettable journey.

Why You’ll Love This Tour

  • Explore ancient ruins and discover the rich cultural tapestry of East Sri Lanka.
  • Relax on pristine beaches, venture through lush jungles, and witness untouched natural beauty.
  • Engage with local communities and experience authentic cultural interactions.
  • Optional water sports in Arugam Bay and wildlife safaris in Gal Oya National Park.

Inclusions

  • Meals: Tailored to Your Preference
  • Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing
  • Accommodation: Hotel (7 nights)
  • Included Activities: Rajagala Monastery, Kumana National Park, Boat Safari in Senanayaka Samudraya, Gal Oya National Park

Itinerary

Arrival – Negombo

Transfer from airport and short city orientation. Relax and prepare for the journey ahead.

Negombo – Wellawaya

Drive: 5 hours. Travel through scenic landscapes and local villages, arriving in Wellawaya for the evening.

Wellawaya – Pottuvil Point

Drive: 2.5 hours. Explore pristine beaches and enjoy optional water sports.

Pottuvil Point

Full day at leisure to enjoy the tranquil coastline or engage in optional excursions.

Pottuvil Point – Yala

Drive: 2.5 hours. Safari adventure in Yala National Park and evening relaxation at the lodge.

Yala – Tangalle

Drive: 1.5 hours. Enjoy the pristine beaches of Tangalle and explore local attractions.

Tangalle – Weligama

Drive: 1 hour. Scenic coastal journey and exploration of local fishing villages.

Weligama – Airport

Drive: 2.5 hours. Departure from Colombo airport with cherished memories.
