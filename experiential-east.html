Overview

From Polonnaruwa to Arugam Bay and Gal Oya, enjoy untouched beauty, local communities and optional water-sports and wildlife.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo (40 minutes)
  • Negombo → Wellawaya (5 hours)
  • Wellawaya → Pottuvil Point (2.5 hours)
  • Pottuvil Point — beach & exploring
  • Pottuvil Point → Yala (2.5 hours)
  • Yala → Tangalle (1.5 hours)
  • Tangalle → Weligama (1 hour)
  • Weligama → Airport (2.5 hours)
