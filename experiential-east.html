Experiential East Tour Overview

Discover the untouched beauty of Sri Lanka’s East Coast. From golden beaches to vibrant local culture, this journey immerses you in authentic experiences.

  • Snorkel in turquoise waters and relax on pristine beaches.
  • Visit historic temples and connect with local villages.
  • Stay in comfortable East Coast hotels with scenic views.
  • Experience eco-friendly and responsible travel options.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Sigiriya – Arrival & relaxation.
  • Sigiriya → Trincomalee – Koneswaram Temple visit.
  • Trincomalee – Pigeon Island snorkeling.
  • Trincomalee – Beach leisure & local village experience.
  • Trincomalee → Pasikuda – Coastal travel & sunset views.
  • Pasikuda – Lagoon, reef, and water activities.
  • Pasikuda → Colombo – Scenic journey back.
  • Colombo → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Accommodation: 7 nights East Coast hotels

Transport: Private A/C vehicle

Activities: Snorkeling, temple visits, beaches, village tours

Meals: Tailored to your preference
