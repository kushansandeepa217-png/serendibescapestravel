Fabulous Sri Lanka Tour Overview

Embark on a journey that combines Sri Lanka’s cultural wonders, wildlife, beaches, and hill country experiences. Perfect for travelers seeking comfort and adventure.

  • Visit iconic sites like Sigiriya, Temple of the Tooth, and Polonnaruwa Ancient City.
  • Experience wildlife on Yala Safari and explore tea plantations in the hill country.
  • Relax on pristine beaches at Bentota and Galle.
  • Enjoy luxury accommodations with curated meals and private transport.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Colombo – City walk & leisure.
  • Colombo → Sigiriya – Evening at Sigiriya.
  • Sigiriya → Polonnaruwa → Sigiriya – Lion Rock & Minneriya Safari.
  • Sigiriya → Kandy – Temple of the Tooth visit.
  • Kandy → Nuwara Eliya – Scenic train ride & tea plantations.
  • Nuwara Eliya → Yala – Safari adventure.
  • Yala → Bentota / Galle – Beach relaxation.
  • Bentota / Galle → Colombo → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Accommodation: 7-night premium stays

Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing

Meals: Tailored to Your Preference

Included Activities: Sigiriya, Polonnaruwa, Temple of the Tooth, Tea Plantations, Yala Safari, Bentota Beaches
