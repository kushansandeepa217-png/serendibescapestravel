Fabulous Sri Lanka Tour

Take the easy way to explore our island paradise and let us handle every detail for you. This journey covers Sri Lanka’s iconic Cultural Triangle, the scenic hills of Kandy and Nuwara Eliya, the jungles of Yala, and the historic Galle Fort — offering a complete Sri Lankan experience.

Why You’ll Love This Trip

  • Immerse yourself in Sri Lanka’s vibrant culture, ancient heritage, and local charm.
  • Journey from misty tea hills to sun-kissed beaches — every view is postcard perfect.
  • Indulge in authentic Sri Lankan cuisine and tropical delights.
  • Experience the island’s wildlife — elephants, leopards, and colorful birdlife.

Is This Trip Right For You?

Perfect for travelers who want the best of Sri Lanka — culture, nature, and relaxation. Whether you’re seeking adventure, photography opportunities, or serene landscapes, this itinerary has something for everyone.

Itinerary

Airport → Dambulla → Habarana

Begin your journey towards Sri Lanka’s Cultural Triangle with a relaxing evening at Habarana.

Habarana → Sigiriya → Polonnaruwa → Habarana

Climb the magnificent Sigiriya Rock Fortress and explore the ancient city of Polonnaruwa.

Habarana → Matale → Kandy

Visit the Matale Spice Garden before arriving in Kandy, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth.

Kandy → Peradeniya → Nuwara Eliya

Enjoy the Royal Botanical Gardens and the scenic drive to the tea country of Nuwara Eliya.

Nuwara Eliya → Ella → Yala

Take in the mountain views on your way to Yala, home of Sri Lanka’s famous leopards.

Yala → Galle → Airport

End your journey exploring the colonial charm of Galle Fort before departure.
