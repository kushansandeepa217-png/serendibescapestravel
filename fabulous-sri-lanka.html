Fabulous Sri Lanka

Explore the highlights of Sri Lanka with our expertly planned 9-day tour, from cultural gems to pristine beaches.

Why You’ll Love This Tour

  • Immerse in Sri Lanka’s rich heritage and local culture.
  • Experience the stunning landscapes, from misty tea plantations to serene beaches.
  • Indulge in authentic Sri Lankan cuisine throughout your journey.
  • Witness wildlife in their natural habitats during guided safaris.

Inclusions

  • Meals: Tailored to Your Preference
  • Transport: Private Air-Conditioned Vehicle for Transfers and Sightseeing
  • Accommodation: Hotel (5 nights)
  • Included Activities: Cave Temple of Dambulla, Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Polonnaruwa, Matale Spice Garden, Temple of the Tooth, Kandy City Tour, Royal Botanical Gardens, Rawana Falls, Yala National Park Safari, Galle Fort & City Tour

Itinerary

Arrival in Negombo

Transfer from Airport to hotel in Negombo. Welcome dinner and briefing.

Negombo – Dambulla – Habarana

Drive: 4.5 hours. Visit cave temples and explore cultural triangle.

Habarana – Sigiriya – Polonnaruwa – Habarana

Drive: 3.5 hours. Explore Sigiriya Rock Fortress and Polonnaruwa ruins.

Habarana – Matale – Kandy

Drive: 2.5 hours. Visit Matale Spice Gardens and Kandy City.

Kandy – Peradeniya – Nuwara Eliya

Drive: 3 hours. Explore Royal Botanical Gardens and scenic tea plantations.

Nuwara Eliya – Ella – Yala

Drive: 5 hours. Enjoy hill country views and wildlife safari in Yala.

Yala – Galle

Drive: 6 hours. Visit historic Galle Fort and nearby beaches.

Galle – Colombo

Drive: 2.5 hours. Explore Colombo City and local markets.

Departure

Transfer to Colombo Airport for departure.
