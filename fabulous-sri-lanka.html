Fabulous Sri Lanka

Take the easy way to explore our island paradise — a well-organised coach tour across the highlights of Sri Lanka.

Back to Tours

Overview

This seat-in-coach tour covers the Cultural Triangle, the hills of Kandy and Nuwara Eliya, Yala National Park and Galle Fort — perfect for first-time visitors wanting an organised, relaxed exploration.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Dambulla / Habarana (4 hours)
  • Habarana → Sigiriya → Polonnaruwa → Habarana (3.5 hours)
  • Habarana → Matale → Kandy (2.5 hours)
  • Kandy → Peradeniya → Nuwara Eliya (3 hours)
  • Nuwara Eliya → Ella → Yala (5 hours)
  • Yala → Galle (6.5 hours)
  • Galle → Airport (2.5 hours)
