Hikkaduwa

The golden shores of Hikkaduwa attract surfers, swimmers, and sunseekers from around the world.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Hikkaduwa Beach is famous for its soft golden sand and clear, turquoise waters.

The Hikkaduwa coral reefs are among Sri Lanka’s most famous marine attractions, known for their vibrant colors and diverse sea life.

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery works to protect endangered sea turtles by rescuing eggs and releasing hatchlings safely into the ocean.

The Hikkaduwa Tsunami Memorial stands as a solemn tribute to the lives lost during the 2004 Indian Ocean tsunami.

Things to Do

  • Explore Jaffna Fort.
  • Temple visits and cultural walks.
  • Visit islands like Delft and Nainativu.
  • Enjoy signature Jaffna cuisine.

Best Time to Visit

Best from January to July — warm and dry weather perfect for sightseeing.
