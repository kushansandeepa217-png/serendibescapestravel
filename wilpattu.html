Wilpattu

Known for leopards, dense forest, and natural lakes across Sri Lanka’s largest national park.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Leopards

One of the best places in Sri Lanka to spot leopards.

Natural Waterholes

Unique “Willus” lakes attracting wildlife.

Birdlife

Rare eagles, owls, and migratory birds.

Safari Trails

Dense forests and long safari routes.

Thick Forest Habitat

Untouched wilderness spanning massive territory.

Things To Do

  • Safari tours
  • Birdwatching expeditions
  • Photography of big cats & wildlife
  • Exploring jungle tracks

Best Time to Visit

February to October — dry season with better wildlife visibility.
