Wilpattu

Wilpattu National Park, famous for leopards and wildlife safaris, boasts scenic waterholes and diverse fauna.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Spot elusive leopards roaming the park, a thrilling experience for wildlife enthusiasts.

Waterholes attract elephants, deer, and birds, providing perfect photography moments.

Birdwatchers can spot endemic and migratory birds across diverse habitats in the park.

Jeep safaris explore park trails and open plains to observe wildlife in natural surroundings.

Lush forests and scrublands create scenic and peaceful landscapes for exploration.

Things to Do

  • Wildlife safaris.
  • Spot leopards and elephants.
  • Birdwatching across the park.
  • Photography of waterholes and forests.
  • Enjoy nature trails.

Best Time to Visit

February to October is ideal for safaris, wildlife sightings, and dry, open conditions.
