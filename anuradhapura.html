Anuradhapura

The sacred ancient capital filled with temples, stupas, and timeless Buddhist heritage.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

This sacred Bodhi tree, over two millennia old, stands as a symbol of peace and devotion, surrounded by tranquil temple gardens where pilgrims gather to worship.

The magnificent Ruwanwelisaya Stupa showcases ancient engineering brilliance, glowing white against the sky as a revered icon of Buddhist spiritual architecture.

Jetavanaramaya, once among the world’s tallest brick structures, dominates the historic skyline and reflects Sri Lanka’s deep cultural and engineering heritage.

The Twin Ponds reveal astonishing ancient hydraulic mastery, forming a serene archaeological gem admired for its symmetry, design, and peaceful surroundings.

Things to Do

  • Visit sacred stupas and temples.
  • Explore royal palace ruins.
  • Cycle through the ancient city.
  • Experience traditional Buddhist rituals.
  • Learn history at museum sites.

Best Time to Visit

December to April is ideal, offering dry weather perfect for temple visits and exploring archaeological sites comfortably.
