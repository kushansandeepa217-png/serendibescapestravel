Everlasting Summer in Lanka

A sun-kissed journey through Sri Lanka’s beaches, mountains & heritage.

Overview

Uncover hidden gems and vibrant landscapes as you explore pristine beaches, ancient ruins, and lush tea plantations. Indulge in authentic local flavors and immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of Sri Lanka.   • Landscapes for Every Mood: From pristine beaches to mist-covered mountains, each day presents new stunning scenery.

  • Comfort & Style: Stay in boutique beach resorts and charming mountain retreats for a perfect mix of luxury and culture.
  • Culinary Adventure: Enjoy fresh seafood, aromatic spices and traditional Sri Lankan dishes every day.

Tour Itinerary

Arrival → Negombo

Begin your summer escape along Sri Lanka’s golden coastline.

Negombo → Anuradhapura

Visit the Ancient City of Anuradhapura — a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Anuradhapura → Sigiriya

Climb the legendary Sigiriya Rock Fortress and explore its royal gardens.

Sigiriya → Trincomalee

Relax by the beach & explore the East Coast’s tropical charm.

Trincomalee

Enjoy snorkeling at Pigeon Island and visit Fort Frederik.

Trincomalee → Koneswaram

Explore Koneswaram Temple, perched dramatically above the ocean.

Koneswaram → Down South

Travel to Southern beaches for a relaxing summer retreat.

Departure

Transfer to the airport with unforgettable summer memories.
