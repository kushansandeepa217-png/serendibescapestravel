Everlasting Summer Tour Overview

Experience Sri Lanka's sun-kissed beaches, turquoise waters, lush greenery, and rich culture. This tour blends coastal relaxation with historic exploration for an unforgettable adventure.

  • Perfect for beach lovers, adventure seekers, and cultural explorers.
  • Immerse in local cuisine, arts, and scenic landscapes.
  • Comfortable travel with private transport and premium accommodations.
  • Eco-conscious and sustainable travel experiences.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Negombo – Relaxing beach evening.
  • Negombo → Bentota – Watersports & lagoon boat ride.
  • Bentota – Full-day leisure at the beach.
  • Bentota → Galle – Explore the UNESCO Galle Fort.
  • Galle – Whale watching & cultural tour.
  • Galle → Mirissa – Beach relaxation & optional activities.
  • Mirissa → Colombo – City tour & shopping.
  • Colombo → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Meals: Tailored meal plan

Transport: Private A/C vehicle

Accommodation: 7 nights in beach & city hotels

Highlights: Galle Fort, Bentota Watersports, Whale Watching, Cultural Tours
