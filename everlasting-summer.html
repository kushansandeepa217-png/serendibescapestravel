Overview

From Anuradhapura to Trincomalee and Passikudah, enjoy a coastal-cultural mix filled with beaches, ruins and marine life.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Colombo (1 hour)
  • Colombo → Anuradhapura (4.5 hours)
  • Anuradhapura → Sigiriya → Minneriya (2 hours)
  • Anuradhapura → Trincomalee (2.5 hours)
  • Trincomalee — beach day
  • Trincomalee — marine excursions
  • Trincomalee → Passikudah (2.5 hours)
  • Passikudah → Colombo (7 hours)
  • Colombo → Airport (45 minutes)
