Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, “Little England”, is famed for its tea estates, cool climate, scenic lakes, and colonial charm nestled in the central highlands.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Gregory Lake offers boating, lakeside walks, and serene scenery surrounded by misty hills and tea plantations.

Explore vast tea estates and learn about Sri Lanka’s iconic Ceylon tea production.

Horton Plains National Park is home to scenic trails, World's End cliff, waterfalls, and endemic wildlife.

Nuwara Eliya Golf Club offers a historic and well-manicured course with stunning highland views.

Stroll through the Victorian-era town, quaint streets, colonial architecture, and local markets for souvenirs.

Things to Do

  • Boating or walking at Gregory Lake.
  • Visit tea estates and factories.
  • Hike Horton Plains and World's End.
  • Golf at historic golf course.
  • Explore the Victorian town and markets.

Best Time to Visit

December to March for cooler weather, clear skies, and ideal trekking, sightseeing, and outdoor activities.
