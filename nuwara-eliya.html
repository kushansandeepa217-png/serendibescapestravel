Nuwara Eliya

The “Little England” of Sri Lanka, known for tea estates, cold climate, lakes, and mountain views.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Tea plantations and factory visits.

Gregory Lake — boating and scenic beauty.

Victoria Park — flowers, birds, and gardens.

Things to Do

  • Explore tea factories.
  • Horse riding and boat rides at Gregory Lake.
  • Walk around town and gardens.
  • Stay in colonial-style hotels.

Best Time to Visit

February to April for clear views and pleasant cold weather.
