Meditation & Yoga Tour Overview

This retreat is perfect for travelers seeking inner peace, wellness, and rejuvenation amidst serene natural surroundings.

  • Daily yoga and meditation sessions guided by experienced instructors.
  • Stay at wellness retreats surrounded by nature in Kandy and Ella.
  • Healthy Ayurvedic meals to rejuvenate mind and body.
  • Optional excursions to cultural sites and waterfalls for a balanced experience.

Itinerary

  • Airport → Kandy – Arrival and relaxation.
  • Kandy – Yoga & meditation sessions.
  • Kandy – Full-day yoga & meditation.
  • Kandy – Excursion to cultural sites and meditation caves.
  • Kandy → Ella – Transfer and mountain retreat.
  • Ella – Waterfall yoga session and nature walks.
  • Ella – Morning meditation, leisure time.
  • Ella → Airport – Departure.

Inclusions

Accommodation: 7 nights wellness retreats in Kandy & Ella

Transport: Private A/C vehicle

Meals: Healthy and Ayurvedic cuisine

Activities: Yoga & meditation sessions, nature walks, cultural visits
