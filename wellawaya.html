Wellawaya

A scenic town with Diyaluma Falls, mountains, and lush farming landscapes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Diyaluma Falls, Sri Lanka’s second highest waterfall, surrounded by greenery.

Explore the scenic ravines and small hiking trails nearby.

Lush tea and farming fields offering photography and local experiences.

Panoramic mountain views ideal for hiking and nature walks.

Multiple small waterfalls around the town providing scenic relaxation spots.

Things to Do

  • Visit Diyaluma Falls.
  • Hiking and trekking in nearby mountains.
  • Explore local farms and landscapes.
  • Nature photography.

Best Time to Visit

December to April — dry season for hiking and sightseeing.
