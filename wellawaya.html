Wellawaya

A gateway to waterfalls, mountain vistas, and unforgettable countryside landscapes.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Diyaluma Falls

Sri Lanka’s second-highest waterfall with natural pools.

Wellawaya Ravine

Dramatic cliffs and striking geological formations.

Farming Fields

Picturesque rural landscapes and rice fields.

Mountain Views

Misty peaks and panoramic highland scenery.

Hidden Waterfalls

Smaller natural falls scattered across the area.

Things To Do

  • Visit Diyaluma Falls upper pools
  • Explore countryside walking trails
  • Photo stops across ravines and cliffs
  • Village dining and local produce

Best Time to Visit

June to September — perfect for exploring waterfalls and hiking.
