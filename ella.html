Ella

A breathtaking mountain escape filled with greenery, waterfalls, and scenic trails.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

The iconic Nine Arch Bridge is a masterpiece of colonial engineering surrounded by lush forest scenery.

Little Adam’s Peak is a must-climb trail offering panoramic mountain views and stunning sunrise moments

Ravana Falls is a dramatic natural waterfall flowing over rocky cliffs into refreshing natural pools.

The train journey to Ella is one of the world’s most beautiful railway routes across tea plantations.

Things to Do

  • Climb Ella Rock and Little Adam’s Peak.
  • Watch sunrise over valleys.
  • Visit waterfalls and caves.
  • Take the scenic train ride.
  • Explore tea plantations and cafes.

Best Time to Visit

January to April brings the best weather for hiking and outdoor scenery.
