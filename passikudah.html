Passikudah

Known for its shallow, calm waters and luxury beachfront resorts — perfect for swimming and relaxation.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Passikudah Beach

One of the longest shallow sandy beaches in Asia.

Resort Strip

Luxury hotels lining the turquoise coastline.

Snorkeling

Crystal-clear waters ideal for reef exploration.

Bay Panorama

Beautiful views of the shallow-water bay area.

Sandbanks

Natural sandbars offering safe swimming zones.

Things To Do

  • Swimming in shallow beaches
  • Snorkeling and diving
  • Sunbathing on pristine sands
  • Resort leisure and spa activities

Best Time to Visit

May to September — perfect weather for beach activities.
