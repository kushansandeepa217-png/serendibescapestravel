Passikudah

Passikudah is a tropical paradise with calm bay waters, pristine sandy beaches, snorkeling spots, and relaxing resorts along the east coast.

Back to Destinations

Key Attractions

Relax on the long, golden sandy beach with gentle waves ideal for swimming and sunbathing.

Stay in luxury resorts offering comfort, spa, dining, and beachside leisure activities.

Snorkel in calm shallow waters, discovering vibrant coral reefs and tropical fish.

Enjoy panoramic views of the wide bay from nearby viewpoints and beach cliffs.

Walk out to the sandbanks during low tide for unique coastal experiences and photography.

Things to Do

  • Relax and swim at Passikudah Beach.
  • Enjoy spa and resort amenities.
  • Snorkel and explore coral reefs.
  • Take panoramic bay walks.
  • Visit sandbanks for unique coastal experiences.

Best Time to Visit

April to September provides calm seas, sunny skies, and perfect conditions for snorkeling and beach activities.
